LSPDFRManager - Quick Start
==========================

1. Launch LSPDFRManager.exe.
2. On first run, follow the Setup Wizard to locate your GTA V directory.
3. Use the 'Browse' tab to find mods on lcpdfr.com.
4. Click 'Install This Mod' on a mod page to queue it.
5. Go to the 'Install' tab to review and execute the installation.

Safe modding!
